About Us

A private school for everyone in Ekurhuleni. To measure the greatness of a school, some would count the pupils. At Faith Academy Christian School we prefer to make the pupils and their parents count. Sometime ago the good Lord has laid in my heart to start a Christian School in our area to cater for all parents who are concern about the upbringing of their children. Part of my mandate from the Lord is to have committed and highly disciplined Educators to help with the raising or our children and at the same time teach them the ways of the Lord.

Vission

The Vision of the School is to train and raise a child in the way of the Lord.

Purpose

The purpose of the school is to ensure that a number of students are trained in maths and science. The existence of this school is to ensure that the moral values of the communities are resuscitated and that we raise a community of Excellency.

The School provides Christian based education from 0 to Grade 12, to children in Boksburg area and surrounds. It uses the Department of Education of South Africa program, as adapted and supplied by School of Tomorrow, to meet the requirements of the South African Core Curriculum.

Faith Academy Christian School also serves as a Christian Institution to assist parents to “train up a child in the way he should go‟ (Proverbs 22:6), in obedience to the commandments of God, according to the Scriptures in the Word of God.

The School endeavours to equip each child with sound Biblically based educational and life skills, as well as Godly wisdom, insight and character, in order to become mature, stable and productive members of society and assist parents of the School to define each child’s mission in Jesus Christ. This process is based on the belief that each and every child has a unique calling in God (Ephesians 1:4-5).

Faith Academy Christian School functions as a Christian ministry and on a not for profit basis
