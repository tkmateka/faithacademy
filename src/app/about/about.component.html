About Us

A private school for everyone in Ekurhuleni. To measure the greatness of a school, some would count the pupils. At Faith Academy Christian School we prefer to make the pupils and their parents count. Sometime ago the good Lord has laid in my heart to start a Christian School in our area to cater for all parents who are concern about the upbringing of their children. Part of my mandate from the Lord is to have committed and highly disciplined Educators to help with the raising or our children and at the same time teach them the ways of the Lord.
